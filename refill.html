Refill FAQs

Got questions? We've got answers.

Do I need to bring my own containers?

Not necessarily! You can use our container library (sterilized glass jars) for a small deposit, or purchase new containers in-store. But bringing your own is the most eco-friendly option!

How does the tare system work?

We weigh your empty containers at the register before you fill. That weight is recorded and subtracted from the final weight, so you only pay for the product you take home.

Is everything organic?

We prioritize organic, fair trade, and locally sourced products. Over 85% of our bulk items are certified organic. Ask our staff for specific product sourcing details!

Can I bring any type of container?

Yes! Glass jars, plastic containers, cloth bags — as long as they're clean and dry. We also accept containers from other stores. Let's keep them out of landfills!

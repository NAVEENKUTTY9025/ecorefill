The Journey to Zero Waste

From a small refill station to a movement — our milestones

2020

The First Refill Station

Started in a 200sq ft space with 15 bulk bins. First month: 47 customers. Today: 12,000+ members strong. The seed of a zero-waste revolution was planted.

2022

Community Workshops Launch

Free composting classes, DIY cleaning product workshops, and school programs reached 3,500+ families. Building a knowledgeable, empowered community.

2024

Second Location & Impact Milestone

Brooklyn store opens. Surpassed 30,000kg plastic waste prevented. Named "Best Sustainable Business" 2024. Doubled our community reach.

2025+

The Circular Future

Expanding to 5 new cities, launching mobile refill trucks, and our proprietary zero-waste certification program. The revolution continues.
